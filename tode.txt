1. Once a player has been dead for two days they will have to be revived by someone crafting and placing a revive head renamed with an anvil, once renamed the head would turn into the head of the player.
2. You can buy auto-revive tokens for half a diamond/netherite armor set which include a diamond/netherite chestplate, and diamond/netherite leggings. You need the two upgrade templates in 1.20+ or four netherite ingots in 1.19.4- which you need for half of the diamond/netherite armor. You can only have five equipped at once with a capacity of ten at once.
